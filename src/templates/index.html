HOW TO PLAY

Welcome to Word Morph! The rule of the game is straightforward: swap one letter from your alphabet hand to turn the active word into another valid English word.

Example Stream:
WORK ➡️ PORK ➡️ PERK

Player A replaced W with P (WORK to PORK). Next, Player B swapped O for E (PORK to PERK).

  • Each player starts with 13 card letters (7 in hand, 6 in draw pile).
  • You have 20 seconds to make a valid swap.
  • If you cannot morph the active word with your current letters, or if your timer runs out, you lose the game!
  • Winning matches grants +25 XP. Level up for every 100 XP gained.
